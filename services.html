Our Services

We are the world's largest university

Appointments with the staff at University Career Services are available for current students and alumni of Longwood. We offer assistance on a variety of fronts and are ready to help! During the academic year, appointments are scheduled between 8:30 a.m. - 5:00 p.m., Monday - Friday. Off-hour appointments may be scheduled on a case-by-case basis with individual Career Coaches.University Career Services does offer walk-in hours, but appointments are recommended. Walk-in hours are first-come, first-served bases and run Monday and Wednesday from 9:00 - 11:00 a.m. and Tuesday and Thursday from 2:00 - 4:00 p.m. University Career Services offers several appointment types with specific learning outcomes associated with each topic and includes tutorials on using important tools like Handshake and the Longwood Network.
